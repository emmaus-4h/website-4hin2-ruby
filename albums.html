mxmtoon has released two EPs and four albums since her first EP, plum blossom in 2018, which was well recieved, especially hit song "feelings are fatal."

In 2019, mxmtoon released her first studio album, the masquerade featuring major hits like "prom dress" which has since gained success on TikTok, being used in a total of more than 400k videos. This album was also the first to feature an acoustic version.

Following this, twin albums dawn and dusk were released in 2020, in April and October.
