Overview

  Mxmtoon AKA Maia, born July 9th 2000, is a American indy-pop singer/songwriter primarily known for her singing, although she also plays ukelele and guitar in many of her songs. mxmtoon first released music at age 17, recording in her parents' California bedroom. She posted covers of pop songs, as well as her own originial confessionals. Today, mxmtoon is a well known musician responsible for viral hits such as Falling for U , a collaboration with Peachy, and TikTok-sensation prom dress.

What does Indie even mean?

Indie used to just mean independent, as in not signed to a label. However, "indie" eventually morphed into a genre, in addition to a definitive term of label status. mxmtoon fits only the genre categorie of indie, seeing as she is signed to the label AWAL.
