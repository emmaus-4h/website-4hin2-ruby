She posted covers of pop songs, as well as her own originial confessionals.
One of mxmtoon's early successes was a feature on Falling 4 U by Peachy, a better-known pop star at the time. From there, she only went up, releasing her first studio album, The Masquerade leading with the song Prom Dress which subsequently went super-viral, especially on TikTok. dawn
