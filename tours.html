mxmtoon has embarked on a remarkable journey, with her touring endeavors beginning after the debut of her EP "plum blossom" in 2019. The plum blossom tour marked the commencement of this musical exploration. In the same year, mxmtoon enchanted audiences with the masquerade tour, coinciding with the release of her debut studio album, "the masquerade." She rounded off the year as the featured artist on Cavetown's tour. Only performances where mxmtoon performed are listed.

In 2020, plans for another tour accompanying the compilation album "dawn & dusk" were set, but unfortunately, it had to be canceled due to the pandemic.

The year 2022 witnessed mxmtoon's return to the stage with the rising tour, a captivating musical experience tied to the release of "the rising" However, it's important to note that the last ten performances of this tour were canceled due to mxmtoon's prioritization of her mental health. mxmtoon released this announcement:

Undeterred, in 2023, mxmtoon embarked on the Bittersweet Daze Tour, a collaborative effort alongside Cavetown, Ricky Montgomery, and grentperez. The tour continued in Europe with other featured artists. Wrapping up the year on a poignant note, mxmtoon treated fans to the plum blossom (revisited) Tour, aligning with the release of her latest album, "plum blossom (revisited)." This journey not only reflects mxmtoon's artistic evolution but also underscores the significance of maintaining mental well-being in the face of the demands of the music industry.
