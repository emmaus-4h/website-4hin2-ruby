mxmtoon has gone on tour four times since her debut EP plum blossom and accompanying plum blossom tour in 2019. She performed the masquerade tour that same year with the release of her debut studio album the masquerade, and closed off the year as the feature artist on Cavetown's tour. Maia planned to go on tour again in 2020, but canceled the tour that was supposed to accompany compilation album dawn & dusk. In 2022, mxmtoon performed the rising tour accompanying the release of the rising. Most recently, in 2023 mxmtoon performed in the collaboration tour Bittersweet Daze along with Cavetown, Ricky Montgomery, and rotating guest artists.
